What is Soundpath?

Soundpath began as a project at RJI's Hack the Future of Journalism event hosted by KQED in San Francisco. The original team included Lo Benichou, Elizabeth Case, Renae Cherry, Celeste LeCompte, Sean Longoria, and Ray Harris. It is now being developed by Celeste LeCompte and Luke Van Horn.
