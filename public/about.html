Soundspot

About us

Soundspot is a project by Celeste LeCompte and Luke Van Horn, two travelers with lots of questions. On cross-country roadtrips, train rides along the East Coast, and adventures around the U.S., we've often looked out the window and wondered about the places we went by. Who lives here? What happens here? Soundspot aims to answer those questions and connect us with stories about the world around us.

We're working with the Public Media Platform API to find local audio stories from public media around the country. We'd love to find more partners. If you have a content API you'd like us to include or are interested in using the Public Media Platform to distribute your content, get in touch!
